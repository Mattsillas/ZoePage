Zoe loves when both of us hold her hands at the same time, because she loves to swing back and forth. The Fog, The Mist, The Buns!

One of our favorite memories of little Zoe was the day we took this picture. It was around 8-o-clock in the morning, and there was a gentle fog all over the ground. Mommy and Zoe

Living the Dream

Zoe is completely attached to her mother, and we just love that about her. She loves to spend time with dad, sure. However it always seems to be mom who she reaches for whenever things aren't quite right. One thing we love about her is how independent she is for a toddler, but mom loves nothing more than when Zoe decides she wants to cuddle. Those times are in short supply, being that Zoe is a brave adventurer and would rather be off accomplishing some great feat. Whenever it happens, it's what we call "living the dream."

One Happy Family

Never Looking Back

Becoming parents was not something we had initially planned for at such a young age, though now we couldn't even imagine life without Zoe! It's so fascinating how life can change forever in an instant. She is such a bundle of fun, and seeing her grow and learn every single day is truly a privelege. Being parents is definitely a challenge at times, but neither of us would trade parenthood for anything. Zoe is our world now, and to us that is beautiful.
